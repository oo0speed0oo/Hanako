# Hanako 花子

> *I forget names. I forget faces. I forget to reach out. Hanako remembers for me.*

Hanako is a personal friendship assistant for iOS. Not a social network. Not a contacts app. A quiet, private tool that helps you stay present in the lives of the people who matter most — before it's too late and they've drifted away.

Built in Swift / SwiftUI by a developer living in Japan. 🇯🇵

---

## 💡 Why I Built This

I've lived in Japan for 15 years. Over time I noticed something: the people I care about most are the ones I forget to reach out to. I forget birthdays. I forget where people live. I forget the last time we spoke. Life gets busy, and friendships quietly fade.

Hanako is my answer to that. A personal assistant that tracks my relationships so I don't have to rely on memory alone.

## ✅ Features

### Available Now
- **Thoughtfulness Dashboard** — who haven't you contacted recently? Who deserves your attention today? Hanako shows you at a glance.

### In Development
- **Gift Log** — remember what you've given, what people love, and what to get them next time
- **Proximity-Aware Map** — see which friends are nearby so you never miss a chance to connect
- **Contacts Integration** — built on top of your existing iOS Contacts, no duplicate entry

### Planned
- Interaction history — log every coffee, call, and catch-up
- Birthday & important date reminders
- Recurring check-in nudges for people you want to stay close to

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Requirements
- Xcode 15+
- iOS 16+
- A Mac to build and run

### Installation

```bash
git clone https://github.com/oo0speed0oo/Hanako.git
open Hanako/Hanako.xcodeproj
```

Hit **Run** in Xcode to launch on simulator or your device.

---

## 💡 Why "Hanako"?

花子 (*Hanako*) is a classic Japanese name meaning "flower child." Friendships are like flowers — they need regular care and attention to flourish. Without it, they fade. Hanako helps you tend the garden.
